TOP FAQS: YOUR GO-TO GUIDE FOR MOST POPULAR QUESTIONS

We recommend contacting us at least 4-6 weeks before your desired moving date, especially during peak seasons like summer, to ensure availability.

A pre-move survey typically takes between 30 to 60 minutes, depending on the size of your home and the number of items to be moved.

Our moving consultant will provide you with a detailed list of prohibited items, which generally includes hazardous materials, perishable foods, and plants.

Insurance provides protection against unforeseen circumstances such as accidents during transit. While we take utmost care, insurance offers an added layer of security and peace of mind.

Insurance coverage is typically based on the declared value of your goods. You can choose from different levels of coverage, from basic liability to full replacement value.

Your main responsibilities include being present during packing and loading, clearly labeling special items, and ensuring all necessary paperwork is completed before the move.

We use a variety of containers, including sturdy cardboard boxes, custom crates for fragile items, and wardrobe boxes for clothing, all designed to protect your belongings.

Packing and loading time varies based on the size of your move. A typical 3-bedroom house can take anywhere from 4 to 8 hours.

Items that cannot be loaded include hazardous materials (like paint and gasoline), firearms, perishables, and pets. Please make separate arrangements for these items.

We offer dedicated vehicle transportation services using specialized car carriers. We can include this service as part of your overall moving plan.
